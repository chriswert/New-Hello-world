Chris Werther

I am the son of a 30 year Air Force Chief Master Sargent. I spent most of my childhood living in either Italy and Germany. After graduation from High School in Germany I came back to Arizona and went to the University of Arizona. There I met my wife of 20 years. I finished my degree with Northern Arizona University while my wife went to Law School at the University of Gonzaga. After returning to Arizona, I started a career in the Golf Industry. I started at the bottom, working as an outside golf attendent. I then moved into the golf shop where I became more familiar with the operations part of a golf course. I also began to teach golf. I eventually was hired by OB Sports where I became an Account Manager. I managed 35 golf course email marketing and website support.
